Research Interests

I study the evolution of ferns at UC Berkeley and I am interested in the role polyploidy has had in the diversification of plants (and not just flowering plants!) Some projects currently underway:
  • Are Ks plots really that convincing when it comes to the oodles of recently inferred paleopolyploidy events?
  • Assessing the evolutionary consequences of transposition in the germline vs. in somatic tissue, why do TEs move like they move?
  • How is a freshly synthesized polyploid genome expressing itself? We're looking at you, our favorite little mustard!
